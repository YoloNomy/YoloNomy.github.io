# Ideal gas

## The fundamental equations

State equation:

$$ PV = n\mathcal{R}T$$

Second's Joule's law:

$$U=U(n,T)$$

## Applications

## Microscopic model

$$PV = \frac{1}{3}n\mathcal{R}T$$

$$ U = \frac{3}{2}n\mathcal{R}T=\frac{3}{2}Nk_BT$$
